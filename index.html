FAQs

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where students from all over the region come together to make their ideas a reality. There are workshops, mentors, snacks, food, swag, and caffeine to keep you going.

Do I need to know how to code?

Nope! We will provide resources, mentors and workshops to support all beginners get started!

How much do I have to pay to attend?

Zero, nada, $0.00, gratis! We'll provide everything you need to have an awesome 24 hours. We will even help you get here with travel reimbursements.

Who can attend?

Anyone enrolled in a highschool, college or university program can attend! Even minors, but we'll need you to sign some extra paperwork.

What should I bring with me?

Anything that you need for a 24-hour hackathon: laptop, charger, toiletries, sleeping bag, and your awesome ideas. Oh, an a Photo ID.

Applications will open soon, enter your email above to stay tuned with updates from HackRGV.
